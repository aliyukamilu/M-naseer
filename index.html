About

Naseer is a brand identity designer with 6years of experience in design industry, a photographer and a digital marketer.

Naseer obtained an online diploma in Graphic Design with focus on Branding from Austin Peay State University in The US, I enjoyed working with Brands like Maiduguri Caps, Mahs Table Tradition and Ayyza Cuisine, Chef Urwat, Sufimart as well as small businesses and start-ups

Naseer loves designing beautiful and simple looking brands and experiences that meet business objectives and delight their users. Naseer does this across multiple touch points to help Brands and companies build strong brand presence and achieve their goals.
